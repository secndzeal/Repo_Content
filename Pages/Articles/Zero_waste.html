10 Tips for a Zero-Waste Kitchen

Published on October 21, 2023

Reducing waste in your kitchen is easier than you think! Here are 10 simple tips to help you get started:

  1. Use reusable containers instead of plastic wrap.
  2. Compost food scraps instead of throwing them away.
  3. Buy in bulk to reduce packaging waste.
  4. Switch to cloth napkins and towels.
  5. Avoid single-use items like paper plates and plastic utensils.
  6. Plan meals to reduce food waste.
  7. Store food properly to extend its shelf life.
  8. Make your own cleaning products.
  9. Shop at farmers' markets for fresh, package-free produce.
  10. Repurpose glass jars for storage.
